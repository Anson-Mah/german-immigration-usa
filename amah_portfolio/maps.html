About My Maps

I have been creating maps for over 3 years. However, I only show the ones made after September of 2023. This is because this is the starting point of a new style of maps I have learned to create. I use a software called QGIS for the fundamental data needed (ex. coastlines, borders, co-ordinate grids, city placements), and another software called paint.net to style everything and to insert extra components such as the legend or compass. You can imagine QGIS as the figurative HTML and paint.net as the figurative CSS.
