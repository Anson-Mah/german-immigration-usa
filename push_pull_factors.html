Antisemitism

Many German Jews left due to antisemitism in Europe.

Freedom of Religion

The right to practice any religion is protected under the U.S. Constitution. German Jews find this useful because they would not be as discriminated for being Jewish as compared to in Germany.

German Revolutions of 1848

Political refugees flee from their homeland upon the failure of the revolutions. The failure of the revolutions showed Germans that political reform in their home country would not be feasible for the foreseeable future.

Freedom of Speech

Freedom of Speech is protected under the U.S. Constitution. Political refugees find this useful because they can voice their political beliefs without having to worry about persecution.

American Recruiters

American recruiters put in lots of effort to bring in new immigrants. The country needed more settlers to make use of the massive amounts of unsettled land and more workers to work in factories.
