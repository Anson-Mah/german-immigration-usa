Beer

German Immigrants built the beer industry. Germans are well known for their beer, and they have brought their signature drink to the United States.

Delis

Delis, also known as Delicatessens, originated in Germany during the 1700s. During the 1800s, German Immigrants popularized the concept of delis in the United States. Now, delis are one of many main traits of an urban environment in the United States.

Young Education

German-born Friedrich Fröbel developed the Kindergarten system, ensuring that children get proper education and develop their skills. The United States has adopted Kindergarten, incorporating it as one of many stages of a child's educational journey.

Christmas Trees

The first Christmas Tree was placed in 1419 in Freiburg. The town bakers decorated the tree with various foods such as fruits and nuts, and the childeren were allowed to remove and eat the foods on New Years Day. Germans had created the tradition of the Christmas tree, and German immigrants brung this tradition with them to the United States.
